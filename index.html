Commands

üéµ Music

+backward - Rewind the song by a custom time or -10 seconds by default.

+bump - Bump a specific song from the queue to the front.

+clear - Clear the Queue!

+forward - Forward the song by a custom time or +10 seconds by default.

+join - Avon joins the voice channel.

+leave - Avon leaves the voice channel.

+loop - Loop the Queue, track or off!

+lyrics - Get the Lyrics of the Current Playing Song.

+pause - Pause the Music!.

+play - Play a song of your favorite choice.

+previous - Play the Previous Music!

+queue - Show the Queue!

+replay - Replay the Music!

+resume - Resume the Music!

+search - search a song of your favorite choice.

+seek - Seek to a specific time in the currently playing song.

+shuffle - Shuffle the Queue!

+singwithme - Sing With Lyrics.

+skip - Skips the Music!

+stop - Stop the Music!
